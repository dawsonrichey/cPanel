ARNIE STANDIFORD

Vice President

BIOGRAPHY

Althjustifyough she is mostly self-taught, artist Arnie Standiford, attributes some of her success to the four instructors she had in the early years. She studied under artists Hazel Pickens, Steve Little, Rowena Preston, and Bill Hancock.

She has enjoyed the camaraderie of other artists like herself while taking part in numerous plain air events. She especially enjoys paintouts at TC Steele and Hanover College.Arnie's work can be seen at the Scott County Museum, The Coffee House, and the Bacala Center in Scottsburg.

In 2009 Arnie received the Mayors award at the annual Madison Art Show, Honorable Mention at the Brown County Art Guild, and Merit Award at the Southern Indiana Center for the Arts. She also had a 75 piece solo exhibit at the Scott County Museum and a 31 piece solo exhibit at the Southern Indiana Center for the Arts that same year.

She is currently serving as vice president of the Scott County Arts Council. You may contact her by calling 752-8739 or through the Arts Council.
